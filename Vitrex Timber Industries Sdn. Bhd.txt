Vitrex Timber Industries Sdn. Bhd.
Since 1991, Vitrex Timber Industries Sdn. Bhd. has been a trusted name in the timber industry, operating with a deep commitment to sustainability and quality. As a family-owned Bumiputra company, we are proud of our heritage and dedication to responsible forestry practices. Our operations are based in Kelantan State, Malaysia, and we work closely with partners in Thailand to manage premium teak plantations.  

With over three decades of experience, Vitrex Timber Industries continues to lead in sustainable timber production, meeting both domestic and international demand for high-quality wood products.


Sustainable Forestry Practices
At Vitrex Timber Industries, sustainability is more than a commitment—it's a way of life. Over the past five years, we have successfully replanted more than 500 hectares of land in Kelantan State. This reforestation initiative is a key part of our effort to maintain a healthy ecosystem while ensuring the continuous supply of premium timber.

Selective Harvesting: We carefully select our timber, ensuring that only trees with a diameter of 100cm or more are harvested. This method allows younger trees to grow and thrive, preserving the natural balance of our forests.

High-Yield Replanting: Our sustainable practices have resulted in an impressive yield of 8 tons per acre, demonstrating our ability to balance environmental responsibility with high production efficiency.

Environmental Certifications: Vitrex Timber is proudly certified by local environmental authorities, and we are actively pursuing FSC (Forest Stewardship Council) certification to further solidify our dedication to responsible forestry.
